Our Heritage

From a small family workshop to the world's premier destination for luxury timepieces

Founded in 1952 by master horologist Edward Imperial, our company began as a small workshop dedicated to the restoration and sale of fine timepieces. What started as a passion for precision and craftsmanship has evolved into the world's most trusted name in luxury watches.

Over seven decades, we have cultivated relationships with the most prestigious watchmakers, from the legendary houses of Switzerland to emerging artisans pushing the boundaries of horological innovation. Each timepiece in our collection is carefully curated, authenticated, and presented with the reverence it deserves.

Today, Imperial Watches stands as a bridge between the rich heritage of traditional watchmaking and the evolving tastes of modern connoisseurs. We don't just sell watches; we preserve and celebrate the art of timekeeping itself.
